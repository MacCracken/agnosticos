## What It Does

- **Social networks**: Small-world (Watts-Strogatz), scale-free (Barabási-Albert), Dunbar layers, homophily, clustering
- **Game theory**: Nash equilibria, prisoner's dilemma, evolutionary stable strategies, tit-for-tat
- **Opinion dynamics**: Voter model, Deffuant bounded confidence, polarization detection, echo chamber index
- **Group dynamics**: Tuckman stages, social loafing (Ringelmann), groupthink risk, collective intelligence
- **Population models**: Logistic growth, SIR/SEIR epidemiology, R₀, herd immunity, demographic transition
- **Social influence**: Conformity thresholds (Asch), innovation diffusion (Bass model), social proof
- **Inequality**: Gini coefficient, Lorenz curve, Pareto distribution, mobility index
