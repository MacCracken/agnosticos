# garjan

> Sanskrit: गर्जन — roar / thunder

Environmental and nature sound synthesis: weather, impacts, water, fire, ambient textures.

- **Version**: 0.1.0
- **Repository**: [github.com/MacCracken/garjan](https://github.com/MacCracken/garjan)
- **Depends on**: hisab 1.2
- **Consumers**: kiran, joshua, dhvani

## Key Capabilities

- Weather: Thunder (distance-based), Rain (4 intensities, Poisson drops), Wind (speed + gustiness)
- Impact: 10 materials, 4 impact types, material-specific resonance and decay
- Water: Stream, Drip, Splash, Waves
- Fire: Crackle (stochastic) + Roar (broadband), intensity-scaled
- Ambient: 6 textures (Forest, City, Ocean, Cave, Desert, Night)
- ~2,000-625,000x real-time
