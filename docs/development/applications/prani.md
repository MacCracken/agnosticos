# prani

> Sanskrit: प्राणी — living being / creature

Creature and animal vocal synthesis: species-specific voice models, call patterns, non-human vocal tracts.

- **Version**: 0.1.0 (v1.0-ready)
- **Repository**: [github.com/MacCracken/prani](https://github.com/MacCracken/prani)
- **Depends on**: svara 1.0, hisab 1.2
- **Consumers**: kiran, joshua

## Key Capabilities

- 13 species (Wolf, Dog, Cat, Lion, Songbird, Crow, Raptor, Snake, Crocodilian, Cricket, Bee, Dragon, Fantasy)
- 4 vocal apparatus types (Laryngeal, Syringeal, Stridulatory, NoiseOnly)
- 14 vocalization types with species-vocalization validation
- 7 behavioral intents with prosodic modifiers
- Species-specific formant targets from vocal tract geometry
- Pitch contours per vocalization, subharmonics, dual-source syrinx, dragon fire-breath
- ~700x real-time
